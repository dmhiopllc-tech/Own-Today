Relapse Prevention

Tools to stay strong during challenges

🚨 Need Immediate Help?

If you're in crisis or need urgent support, reach out now.

🛡️

Identify Triggers

Recognize situations, people, or emotions that may challenge your recovery. Being aware is the first step to prevention.

📞

Emergency Contacts

Quick access to your support network. Sponsor, counselor, family, and crisis hotlines - all in one place.

🧠

Coping Strategies

Healthy ways to handle stress, cravings, and difficult emotions. Practice these tools regularly.

⚠️

Warning Signs

Learn to recognize early warning signs of relapse. Early intervention can prevent a full relapse.

🗺️

Safety Plan

Your personalized step-by-step action plan for when cravings or triggers arise. Know exactly what to do.

💪

Success Stories

Read inspiring stories from others who overcame similar challenges. You're not alone in this journey.
