Warning Signs

Watch for These Red Flags:
  • Isolating from support network
  • Skipping meetings or therapy sessions
  • Romanticizing past substance use
  • Increased irritability or mood swings
  • Poor self-care (sleep, eating, hygiene)
  • Lying or being dishonest
  • Visiting old using locations
  • Feeling overconfident about recovery

Emergency Action Plan

  1. STOP - Pause and acknowledge the craving or trigger
  2. BREATHE - Take 10 deep breaths to calm your nervous system
  3. CALL - Contact your sponsor, counselor, or crisis line (988)
  4. LEAVE - Remove yourself from the triggering situation immediately
  5. ATTEND - Go to a meeting (in-person or online) right now
  6. JOURNAL - Write down what you're feeling and why
  7. REFLECT - Remember your reasons for recovery and consequences of use
  8. REWARD - Once safe, celebrate staying sober with a healthy reward
